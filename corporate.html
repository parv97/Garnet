Corporate Tax Rates Comparison (as of 2024)

Country Statutory Corporate Tax Rate Effective Tax Rate (for large companies) Additional Information
India 25% or 30% (depending on turnover) 30.9% or 34.94% (including surcharge and cess) Reduced rate of 22% available for certain companies meeting specific conditions.
UK 25% (for profits exceeding £250,000) 19% (for profits below £50,000), sliding scale for profits between £50,000 and £250,000 Surcharge of 10% applies for taxable income exceeding £10 million.
US 21% (federal) Varies by state (average around 26%), some states offer additional deductions and credits Federal rate will decrease to 20.5% in 2025.
Canada 15% (federal) 26.5% (average combined federal and provincial rate) Lower rates available for small businesses and specific industries.
Australia 30% 30% Reduced rate of 25% available for small businesses.
