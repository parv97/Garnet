What is GST?

GST, or Goods and Services Tax, is a consumption tax levied on the supply of goods and services. This multi-stage tax is designed to eliminate cascading effects and create a more efficient taxation system. At Garnet Professional Pvt Ltd, we adhere to the GST regulations to contribute to a transparent and accountable business environment.

Our GST Registration

Garnet Professional Pvt Ltd is proud to be a GST-registered entity. Our GST registration ensures compliance with tax laws and enables us to seamlessly pass on the benefits of input tax credits to our clients.Cross-Border Tax Compliance

Input Tax Credits

As a responsible business, Garnet Professional Pvt Ltd is entitled to claim input tax credits on the GST paid for inputs and services used in our operations. This practice prevents the duplication of GST throughout the supply chain, ultimately benefiting our clients.

International Clients and GST

For our international clients, it's important to understand the impact of cross-border transactions on GST. Depending on your location and the nature of our services, specific provisions, such as reverse-charge mechanisms, may apply. We recommend consulting with your tax advisor for personalized guidance.
